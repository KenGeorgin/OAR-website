OUR STORY

My husband and I both were software engineers by profession and we quit our job and started a business which we both are passionate about – A business which deals with plants and trees, create a greener surrounding by simple words.

Why did I quit. It begins from my early age of life. I hailed from a small village in Kerala. During my child-hood, travelling in a car was luxury. TV was not popular in our place and of course there were no mobile phones. Packet milk was not there, but most of the houses were have a cow or a goat. We kids used to play in the ground or paddy fields and drink water from the nearby well (not the boiled water or distilled water)...
