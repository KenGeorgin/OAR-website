OAR farm

Everything we do, we believe in improving human health by including organic food in our diet. We believe in improve quality of our life by accepting farming/agriculture as a hobby. We are introducing a mobile app that enables anyone to start doing farming and cultivating organic food in a rented farm-unit. A beautifully designed simple to use mobile app which enable you to lease a small farm-unit and cultivate the vegetables you prefer. We just happened to introduce OAR-farm.

Everybody who lives in the city or town loves to have their own farm or have good organic food. But how is it possible for a person who lives in an apartment. On the other hand, there are many organic farmers who are producing good organic products, but not getting enough money for their hard work. OAR-farm will connect these two groups and create a win-win situation for both.
